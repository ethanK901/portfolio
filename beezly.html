Case Study

Beezly Membership Platform

End-to-end product build for Beezly, delivering a subscription-based mobile membership experience that helps lifestyle coaches launch and grow paid communities.

Start a project Back to highlights

Project Overview

Partnered with the founding team to turn a coaching concept into a production-ready product. The MVP shipped across iOS and Android in under four months, balancing rapid iteration with maintainable code structure for future growth.

  • Defined user journeys, low-fidelity prototypes, and release roadmap alongside stakeholders.
  • Implemented onboarding, profile creation, and member segmentation for personalized content feeds.
  • Integrated Stripe for recurring payments, eligibility checks, and automated receipts.

Impact

  • Launched beta with 15 coaches and 1,200 members while maintaining sub-second load times.
  • Reduced manual admin tasks by 60% through real-time dashboards and push notification workflows.
  • Established analytics tracking for retention, churn, and revenue cohorts to inform roadmap decisions.
